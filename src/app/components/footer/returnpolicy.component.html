Cancellation and Return Policy

DealsBargain allows you to cancel the ordered product before the item was delivered. If the payment is done through Online, the amount you paid will be credited to your account in 7 working days.

 

DealsBargain also allows you to return the delivered product only in the following cases:

  • You are not satisfied with the quality of the product
  • Delivered product was different from the ordered product
  • Product was delivered beyond the expected delivery time
