About Us

DealsBargain is an associate company of Itech Reach Inc. whose primary area of business is Information Technology, which was established in 2011 in the United States. It offers valuable IT services to various well known MNC clients.

The core team of Itech Reach Inc., has envisaged DealsBargain as a business entity, which was incorporated in 2016. It ventured into e-commerce and has been successfully launched in the United States. It shortly plans to expand internationally (Canada, Germany, Australia, Ethiopia and India).

DealsBargain exists with an aim to minimize demand-supply gap in a wide variety of consumer goods and its allied services between sellers and buyers in various geographical locations. It establishes a win-win situation for both ends, which ensures improved sales deals for sellers and best shopping deals experience for buyers.  

Here at DealsBargain, we are committed to bring the best deals at your fingertips without having to search through various vendor sites.
